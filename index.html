About Our School

Natabari High School (H.S.), established in 1967, is a government-aided co-educational institution located in Debottar Charaljani, Natabari I, under the Cooch Behar district of West Bengal. Affiliated with WBBSE and WBCHSE, our school offers classes from V to XII, including both Arts and Science streams at the Higher Secondary level.

Surrounded by lush greenery and a peaceful rural atmosphere, our school provides the perfect environment for learning and character development. We emphasize academic excellence, moral values, and overall personality growth. Through our active NSS unit and various co-curricular activities, we nurture responsible and socially aware citizens of tomorrow.

  • ✔ WBBSE & WBCHSE affiliated school with Arts & Science streams
  • ✔ Dedicated and experienced faculty members
  • ✔ Co-curricular engagement through NSS and various social activities
  • ✔ Focus on discipline, moral education, and community values

Admission Process (Classes V-XII)

For Classes V-IX

  • 1 Registration opens in January each year
  • 2 Age criteria: Class V (10-11 years), Class VI (11-12 years), etc.
  • 3 Admission through lottery system if applications exceed seats

For Class XI

  • 1 Admission after Madhyamik results are declared
  • 2 Streams available: Science and Arts
  • 3 Based on merit and seat availability

Documents Required

For All Classes

  • Birth Certificate
  • Cast Certificate
  • Aadhar Card
  • Previous year's report card
  • 2 passport size photos

Additional for Class XI

  • Madhyamik mark sheet
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Stream selection form

Important Notes

  • Admission fee: ₹240 for Classes V-IX, ₹300 for Class XI
  • Forms available at school office from December 15th
  • Last date for submission: January 15th (Classes V-IX)
  • Class XI admission begins after Madhyamik results
  • Automatic promotion for Class IV students of attached primary school
