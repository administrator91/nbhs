About Our School

Natabari High School (H.S.), established in 1967, is a government-aided co-educational institution located in Debottar Charaljani, Natabari I, under the Cooch Behar district of West Bengal. Affiliated with WBBSE and WBCHSE, our school offers classes from V to XII, including both Arts and Science streams at the Higher Secondary level.

Surrounded by greenery and a peaceful rural atmosphere, our school provides the perfect environment for learning and character development. We focus on academic excellence, moral values, and all-round personality growth. Through our active NSS unit and co-curricular programs, we ensure students grow into responsible and aware citizens of tomorrow.
